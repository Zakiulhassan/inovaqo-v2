The Concept 2.0 dashed continuity line — round lime dashes on a meandering path. Use it to express movement, continuity, and creativity: traveling over imagery, underlining a key phrase, connecting two moments.

```jsx
<DashedPath preset="swoosh" height={40} />
<DashedPath preset="loop" animate />
```

Rules: one per composition · always lime (mist on lime backgrounds) · round caps · it should lead the eye somewhere, never decorate aimlessly. The exact hand-drawn vectors from the Figma concept live in `assets/motifs/` (use `.inq-dash-loop` / `.inq-dash-wave` CSS mask utilities to recolor).
