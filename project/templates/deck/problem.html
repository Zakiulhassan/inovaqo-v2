The Problem / 02

Most AI products do not fail because the idea was bad.

They fail because the use case is unclear, the architecture is weak, the data is disconnected, or the product cannot scale after launch.
Risk / A
Unclear AI value

Workflow, user need, and outcome were never defined.

Risk / B
Slow execution

Stuck in planning, hiring, or vendor handoffs.

Risk / C
Fragile architecture

Fast builds become expensive rewrites.

Risk / D
Black-box delivery

Founders cannot see progress or trust the path.
